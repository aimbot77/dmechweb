[01] The Problem

Human Operators
Cannot Scale

Swarm-class drone threats saturate human decision capacity. The coordination gap between detection and response determines survivability.

Current counter-drone operations rely on human operators making engagement decisions under pressure. Response times of 3–8 seconds per target and a practical ceiling of 3–5 simultaneous tracks create a critical bottleneck when facing coordinated drone swarms.

AEGIS-X eliminates this bottleneck. The system processes sensor data, evaluates threats, assigns effectors, and enforces rules of engagement in under 10 milliseconds — operating at speeds that match the threat, not the operator.
